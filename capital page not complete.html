Atlanta not only the capital of Georgia, but it is a melting pot for art, culture, and fun.
It is home to the World of Coca-Cola, the Center for Civil and Human Rights,
and the Georgia Aqaum. Not only does it have a long history of hip-hop, but it
the spirit of such music is alive and well in the streets of Atlanta today.
Moreoever, Atlanta is a happy place for talented musicinas
including singers, pianists, and actors. It is home to the Fox Theatre and the High Musuem
which not only include varying acts but also some of the most seasoned artists.
Atlanta also has many shopping centers including Ponce City Market that not only hosts
reknown brands but also local businesses. These business range from food from
unique countries, hand-made clothing, and artwork. Atlanta is also incredibly
welcoming to those from all walks of life regardless of age, gender, country of origin,
sexual orientation, and race. Atlanta is a city in Georgia
that is truly one of a kind that many don't forget.
