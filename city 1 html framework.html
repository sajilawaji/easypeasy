Stone Mountain is an incredibly unique and small city located in Georgia.
It is home to the State Park of Stone Mountain which has many attractions
including a trolley, several trails, a lazer show each weekend, and a ferry.
Stone Mountain was originally occupied by Native Americans also known as
Creek American Indians before European settlers came and forcibly
took their land. Although these settlers took this land, today
Stone Mountain is open to all and home to many residents of
the immigrant community who reside in the area and in a nearby town
named Clarkston. Stone Mountain is an incredibly diverse and welcoming city in Georgia.
