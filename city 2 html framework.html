Decatur is an eclectic city in Georgia which a gathering point for many creatives.
It is home to the Fernbank Musuem of Natural History as well as many other museums.
It has several parks which host several events including exciting festivals, concerts,
and film screenings. It also has several options for vegans, vegetarians
and meat-lovers who appreciate fine-dining and local resturants.
When passing through the city, whether by foot or in a car, passerbys
will witness the incredible and colorful murals painted by
locals of this city and Atlanta.
Decatur is a city in Georgia that is a mecca for artists and those who appreciate their work.
